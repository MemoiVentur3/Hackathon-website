About Us

OVERVIEW OF PROJECT

The Memoi Africa and Telos Bootckathon is an initiative that leverages Telos Blockchain and Web3 technologies to confront and overcome real-world challenges. This project brings together a diverse group of participants who will collaborate to develop innovative solutions that address the pressing issues, all built on the Telos Network.

This program will consist of a comprehensive training bootcamp, followed by a hackathon where participants will have the opportunity to apply their skills to hack for prizes, sponsored by Telos. Through this Bootcathon, we aim to equip participants with the knowledge and skills required to create impactful solutions that make a positive impact using Telos Network.

Implementation

STAGE I

Bootcamp

A specialized bootcamp focused on Telos Blockchain and Web3 application development. In-depth training covering Telos architecture, consensus mechanisms, cryptography, and development tools.


  • Practical labs and hands-on experience.
  • Team formation and project planning.
  • Technical workshops to enhance participants' skills.

STAGE II

Hackathon

The hackathon, themed "Transforming Everyday Challenges with Telos EVM," will be conducted virtually, where teams of up to 5 members will collaborate to tackle real-world challenges. With an emphasis on fostering creativity and originality, participants will develop projects that directly address the specified theme. These projects will be evaluated based on their innovation, technical complexity, design quality, potential for meaningful impact, and alignment with the overarching theme of the hackathon. Furthermore, to encourage diversity and inclusion, each team must include a minimum of 1 female participant.

STAGE III

Awards and Bounties

During the hackathon, we'll recognize and reward the top-performing teams with exciting prizes. These awards will span various categories, including Best Technical Solution, Most Impactful Idea, and Best Team Collaboration. The submitted projects will undergo rigorous evaluation based on specific criteria, encompassing innovation, technical intricacy, design, potential societal impact, and alignment with the event's overarching theme.
